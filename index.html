Our menu draws on the Italian and Syrian roots of our chef: from mouth-watering falafel burgers and creamy hummus, to fish based takes on schnitzel and shawarma.

We bring our neighborhood together over mint tea by day and transform into a candlelit dining experience with good vibes by night.
